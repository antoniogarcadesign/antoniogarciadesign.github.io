I'm Antonio Garcia — a multidisciplinary graphic designer and motion artist based in the DMV area. I graduated from Bowie State University with a B.A. in Visual Communication & Digital Media Arts, Advertising & Design.

My work spans two worlds. Professionally I create motion graphics, technical infographics, and visual communications for defense technology. Creatively I build cinematic brand experiences for artists, musicians, and entertainment projects through my studio Echolume.

I hold an active Secret Security Clearance and bring the same precision and craft to every project — whether it's a theatrical film poster or an Air Force compliance roadmap.
